Endbands

Medium-Fidelity Prototype

Endbands attempts to simplify the difficulties of keeping track of one's reading progress.

It's goal is to be simple, fast, and increasingly intuitive, minimizing the commitments of time and attention. Endbands aims to promote a movement between reading, documenting, and further engagements that can happen fluidly, in a non-disruptive manner.
